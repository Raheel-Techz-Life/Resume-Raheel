# Key Observations and Solutions in Aadhaar Enrolment & Authentication

**Meeting Date:** January 9, 2026  
**Owner:** @DishaRaikar15

---

## 1. Senior Citizens – Biometric Failure

### Observation:
Fingerprint authentication often fails for senior citizens due to aging-related issues

### Solution:
Introduce a **nominee-based secondary authentication**, where a trusted family member can authenticate when biometrics fail

---

## 2. Twins with Similar Biometrics

### Observation:
In rare cases, twins may have highly similar fingerprints and retinal patterns, making Aadhaar authentication difficult

### Current Status:
⚠️ **No solution identified**

---

## 3. Rural Area Accessibility

### Observation:
Limited internet access and lack of smartphones make Aadhaar enrolment and updates difficult in rural areas

### Solution:
- Use **ASHA workers** for initial registration and awareness
- Deploy **mobile Aadhaar vans** for doorstep enrolment

---

## 4. Impersonation and Biometric Misuse

### Observation:
Cases of identity impersonation were identified where different individuals had matching biometric data

### Current Status:
⚠️ **No finalized solution yet** – requires stronger verification and security mechanisms
